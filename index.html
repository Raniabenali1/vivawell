Welcome to VIVAWELL

Who Are We?

VIVAWELL is a non-governmental organization (NGO) dedicated to promoting health and well-being in line with the United Nations' Sustainable Development Goal (SDG) for Good Health and Well-being.

Our mission is to ensure equitable access to healthcare, encourage healthy lifestyles, and create a supportive community for both physical and mental well-being.

Our Focus:

  • Better Access to Healthcare: Especially in underserved rural areas.
  • Mental Health Support: Raising awareness and offering essential services.
  • Encouraging Healthy Lifestyles: Promoting active living and balanced nutrition.
